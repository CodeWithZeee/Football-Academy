Fundamentals

The Rules of
The Game.

01

11 Players

Each team fields eleven players, working in precision across the pitch in a structured formation.

02

90 Minutes

Two intense 45-minute halves. A battle of endurance, concentration, and controlled aggression.

03

No Hands

Outfield players rely solely on feet, chest and head. Hands are the keeper's exclusive domain.

04

Offside Law

A tactical rule preventing attackers from lurking behind the final defensive line for easy goals.

05

Fouls & Free Kicks

Dangerous or illegal play is punished, granting dead-ball opportunities to the aggrieved team.

06

Goal Scoring

The entire ball must cross the goal line. That fleeting moment is worth everything.
